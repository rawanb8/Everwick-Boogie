Our Mission

To create handcrafted, eco-friendly candles that transform any space into a sanctuary of warmth and comfort, while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and ethical practices.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 during a time when homes became more important than ever, EverwickBoogee began as a passion project to bring comfort and tranquility to people's lives. What started in a small workshop has grown into a commitment to creating the finest handcrafted candles while supporting sustainable practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your candles burn?

+

Our candles have varying burn times depending on size: Small (6 oz) burns 25-30 hours, Medium (10 oz) burns 45-50 hours, Large (16 oz) burns 70-80 hours, and Extra Large (24 oz) burns 100-120 hours.

Are your candles made from natural ingredients?

+

Yes! We use 100% natural soy wax, cotton or wood wicks, and premium fragrance oils. All our ingredients are eco-friendly and safe for indoor use.

Can I create a completely custom candle?

+

Absolutely! Our customization page allows you to choose from different scents, colors, sizes, containers, and wick types. You can also contact us for special requests or bulk custom orders.

Do you offer international shipping?

+

Currently, we ship within the United States. We're working on expanding international shipping options. Please contact us if you're interested in international orders.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your candle, contact us for a full refund or exchange. Custom candles may have different return conditions.
